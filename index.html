Welcome to NBA World memory card matching game. Please choose one of the options below:

The objective of the game is to match all cards on the board before the time runs out. A match is made if a player identifies 2 flipped cards that are identical. If the card's do not match then the cards will flip back over. There are 3 difficulty levels available for a player to test themselves if they find the game to easy:

  • Easy - 18 cards
  • Medium - 24 cards
  • Hard - 30 cards
